Glass

I blow glass because I love the material, the science, and the art. I started glassblowing at the MIT Glass Lab and have taken classes in Boston and the SF bay area. I have also been to the Pilchuck Glass School (2024, 2018) and Haystack Mountain School of Crafts (2022, 2016, 2012), where I lived/breathed/worked in glass.
